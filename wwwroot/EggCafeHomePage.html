HUEVOS RANCHEROS

Grilled chicken, avocado, roasted corn, black beans, Monterey Jack cheese, pomegranate seeds, and toasted pepitas on a bed of fresh mixed greens tossed in a creamy ranch dressing.

FRESH BEET SIDE SALAD
Chilled red beets and fresh orange tossed in a medley of kale and baby spinach with a lemon and herb vinaigrette, topped with crumbled goat cheese and toasted pepita seeds.

SANDWICHES
